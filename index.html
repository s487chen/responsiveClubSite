Popcorn Game Club

Enjoy Competitive Games?

Bring your laptop or just a controller, join the game! Beat other talented gamers to win a weekly prize. Popcorn club offers all kinds of game events.

Just wanna hang out?

Bring your soft drinks and popcorn, sitting on the coach discussing games with other passinate gamers. Popcorn club is a place for gamers to hang out. You can meet new friends here and watch people showing off their crazy game moves.
